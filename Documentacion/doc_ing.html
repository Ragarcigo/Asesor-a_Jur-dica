Legal Services

At Asesorías y Abogados García, we offer a complete range of legal services to resolve any legal questions or conflicts you may have. Our services include:

  1. Preventive legal advice
  2. Defense in trials and litigation
  3. Consulting on contracts and agreements
  4. Mediation and conflict resolution
  5. Consulting in business law
  6. Family law consulting
  7. Criminal law consulting
Each case is unique, so we offer a personalized approach adapted to the client's needs.

How does it work?

  1. Initial consultation: At your first consultation, one of our expert lawyers will listen to your case and explain how we can help you. This consultation can be in person or virtual, depending on your preferences.
  2. Legal Diagnosis: After evaluating your situation, we will provide you with a legal strategy adapted to your needs.
  3. Continuous work: If you decide to hire our services, we will keep you informed at all times about the progress of your case.
  4. Resolution: The objective is to provide you with an effective solution, whether through agreements, conciliations or judicial procedures.

Consulting Process

  1. Schedule an appointment: You can schedule your appointment through our website or by phone.
  2. Initial consultation: This consultation will determine the nature of the legal problem and offer options.
  3. Action plan: A detailed plan will be presented with the steps to follow, associated costs and estimated deadlines.
  4. Continuous support: The García Advice and Lawyers team will provide you with assistance throughout the entire legal process.
